Bag everything before you travel

Welcome to TravelBag!

Whether your travel is domestic or international, TravelBag is here to help you prepare. Travel Bag helps you create a checklist to ensure everything needed for your travel is packed.

Answer a series of travel questions to generate your customized checklist. TravelBag can save incomplete checklists for future updating, and saves all completed checklist.


Custom Checklist


A custom Travel Bag checklist is generated based on your response to the questionnaire and will include location specific items needed and recommended for your trip.

  • Visa information (international travel)
  • vaccination information for (international travel)
  • Basic Travel Checklist items
