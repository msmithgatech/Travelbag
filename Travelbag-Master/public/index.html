A travel checklist and More!

Bag everything before you travel

Welcome to TravelBag!

Whether your travel is for business or personal reasons, both domestic or international, Travel Bag is here to help. Travel Bag will help you create a checklist to ensure everything needed for your travel is packed.

You will be asked a series of travel questions that will be used to generate your checklist. Travel Bag currently offers a Basic Checklist and a Custom Checklist. Travel Bag can save your checklists for future use in the "Travelbags" section if you create an account.


Basic Checklist

A Basic Travel Bag checklist is generated based on your response to the questionnaire and will include items normally packed for a trip.

  • Personal care items
  • Clothing specific to travel
  • Electronic needs

    • Custom Checklist

    A custom Travel Bag checklist is generated based on your response to the questionnaire and will include location specific items needed and recommended for your trip.

  • Visa information (international travel)
  • vaccination information for (international travel)
  • Basic Travel Checklist items
